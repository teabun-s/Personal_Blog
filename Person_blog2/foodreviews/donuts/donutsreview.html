Happy Donuts San Francisco

Summary: These donuts truly make me happy. Have extra change jingling around in your pocket? Then drop by Happy Donuts and get a $2 donut. I've had the cream-filled donut, the cake donut, and the apple fritter. Each is gigantic, lusciously doughy on the inside, and the fritter has real life apples inside! Delicious with coffee or tea, though I prefer these as a late night snack, which is convenient since they're open 24/7. It's cash only!
