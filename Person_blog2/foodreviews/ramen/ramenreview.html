Tsujita Artisan Noodle LA Koreatown

Summary: I wasn't sure if I'd like this, but boy I sure did. This is my first time trying dipped ramen and I'm a fan. I still prefer ramen all in one bowl but I see how this is a thing. The noodles were nice and chewy and take on the very rich broth when dipped. The broth could've been a little hotter because you're dipping cold noodles in a warm broth. Over time the broth gets cold and can congeal, so you want to eat it fairly quickly. If you want to drink the broth they will ask you and they will dilute it and heat up the broth for you.
